Mini-Tati-Helpher

Description

Our primary aim is to focus on those elderly people who suffer from osteoarthritis, myasthenia gravis, etc. We provide them medicine on time by means of automated Medbot. We enable Bluetooth module to track their movements and also to monitor them even with smart phones. In our prototype we have provided necessary space to place medicine and also provide water. We had installed necessary sensors which can detect the surrounding and can move around without having any trouble. Extra provision is available for keeping other things like shoes ,spectacles,etc..

Component Used

1. Bluetooth Module
2. Ultrasonic Module
3. Arduino Nano, Arduino UNO
4. Battery and Sevo motor

De-Pollution

Description

The air we breathe is becoming increasingly polluted. It is mainly caused by Pollutants released by motorist vehicles such as (CO), (CO2), (NOx),(HC), particulate matter (PM10). It is estimated that 9 out of 10 people are affected by polluted air, and globally, toxic pollutants exceed average annual values. The motto of our project is to save people from toxic gases released by vehicles and increase the air quality.

Component Used

1. MQ7 Gas Sensor
2. SSD1306 OLED display
3. Arduino Nano
